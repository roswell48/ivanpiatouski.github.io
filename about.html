About Ivan

My Story

I'm Ivan Piatouski, a career coach with over a decade of experience helping professionals at all levels land better jobs and negotiate higher compensation. Throughout my career, I've worked with hundreds of candidates—from early-career professionals to senior executives—helping them navigate the entire job search and negotiation process with confidence.

My passion is simple: ensuring people get paid what they're worth and land in roles that truly fit their career goals. I've seen firsthand how the right guidance can transform careers and unlock financial potential that many professionals didn't even know was possible.

My Expertise

  • Salary negotiation tactics proven to work with Fortune 500 companies and startups
  • Job search strategy that gets you multiple competing offers
  • Interview preparation and coaching for confidence and success
  • Compensation package evaluation and optimization
  • Negotiating raises, promotions, and improved working conditions
  • Executive compensation and equity negotiation

My Philosophy

Negotiation isn't about being aggressive or difficult—it's about clearly communicating your value and working collaboratively toward mutually beneficial outcomes. I teach ethical, relationship-preserving negotiation tactics that strengthen your professional relationships while securing better deals.

I believe every professional deserves access to expert guidance on career decisions. Whether you're just starting out or leading a team, you should have the knowledge and tools to advocate for yourself effectively.
